Icons: Application, Feature, Interface

Cloudy, Upbeat, Energy, Recognizable, Simple

The SoundCloud application is the main icon worked with for this project. The two main components were the sound bars that make up the left half of the SoundCloud icon, and the cloud that took up the right half.

Striking, Energetic, Flashy, Simple, Relatable

The Stream feature allows users to check who recently uploaded music to SoundCloud. The main part was the lightning bolt and messing around with different appearances. The other part was the background and making it flash and change colors as well.

Quick, Vibrant, Fast, Simple, Relatable

The Play Button interface is a quick way for SoundCloud users to play or pause their music. The main two icons here were the play and pause button. I wanted to coordinate a little message with the color scheme and the fundamental meaning behind the play and pause buttons.
